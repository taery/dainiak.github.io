Полезные материалы

  • Graph Drawing Symposia
  • Planarity game. You are offered a planar graph, drawn initially with many edge crossings. Your goal is to untangle it.
  • Treevis.net — A Visual Bibliography of Tree Visualization.
  • Timevis.net — A Visual Survey of Visualization Techniques for Time-Oriented Data.
  • WebBrain mind mapping software example.
  • Cytoscape network analysis and visualization software with a web-oriented version Cytoscape.js
  • Gephi network analysis and visualization software.
  • GDToolkit — an object-oriented C++ library for handling and drawing graphs.
  • GraphViz — graph visualization software.
  • yWorks diagrammming software company. See demo 1 and demo 2.

Литература

  • G. Di Battista, P. Eades, R. Tamassia, I. G. Tollis. Graph Drawing: algorithms for the visualization of graphs. Prentice-Hall, 1999.
  • U. Brandes. The Left-Right Planarity Test. 2009.
  • I. F. Cruz, R. Tamassia. Graph Drawing Tutorial, 1994. See also Part 1 and Part 2.
  • M. Kaufmann, D. Wagner (editors). Drawing Graphs: Methods and Models. Springer, 2001.
  • L. Lovász. Geometric Representations of Graphs. Institute of Mathematics Eötvös Loránd University, Budapest. 2009.
  • T. Nishizeki, S. Rahman. Planar Graph Drawing. World Scientific Publishing, 2004.
  • H. C. Purchase, D. Carrington, J-A. Allder. Empirical evaluation of aesthetics-based graph layout. Empirical Software Engineering, 7, 233–255, 2002.
  • K. Sugiyama. Graph Drawing and Applications for Software and Knowledge Engineers. World Scientific Publishing, 2002.
  • R. Tamassia (editor). Handbook of Graph Drawing and Visualization. CRC Press, 2007.
